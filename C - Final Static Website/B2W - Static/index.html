Your PT Jamie McGonigal

Fully certified, experienced and available for one on one or group sessions!

I became a personal trainer partly due to frustration of so many people not getting the quality they deserve this is where I set myself away from the majority and actually educate my clients on training and nutrition by no means will I be a rep counter. My main aim is to help as many people as possible get into the gym and start feeling better about themselves.

Everyone has to start somewhere. It is my passion to give you the necessary tools needed to be a better version of you through exercise and education thus increasing your quality of life.

I’m a fairly easy going person although if I have a task at hand it must be done with the upmost precision.

I’ve been involved in the health and fitness industry for the last ten years mainly working as a fitness instructor. During this time I also stepped into the role of an exercise referral instructor. I’ve tried almost every sport there is and various different training methods although my favourite would be Olympic weightlifting.
